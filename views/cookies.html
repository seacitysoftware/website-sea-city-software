This website puts small text files, known as cookies, onto your computer. The cookies are used to make this website work and to collect information on how you use this website. Be sure to also read our privacy policy.

What cookies are in use?

Security

We use a cookie in order to prevent action on this website (such as submitting a contact form) from being used from a different website. This is an essential cookie.

The following cookie is set:

  • csrf

Ensuring you are not a robot with Google ReCAPTCHA

We make use of Google ReCAPTCHA in order to ensure the actions users take on this website are not that of software known as a "robot", or simply "bot". These are essential cookies.

The following cookies are set:

  • PREF
  • NID
  • HSID
  • APISID
  • SID

Cookie message

On visiting this website we show a message which informs you that cookies are used. A cookie is used to check if you have already seen the message, so it is not shown again. This is an essential cookie.

The following cookie is set:

  • EU_COOKIE_LAW_CONSENT
